Welcome

Africa is home to a vibrant and diverse musical tradition. From the resonant beat of the djembe to the soothing melodies of the kora, each instrument tells a story of culture and history.

Featured Instruments

Djembe

A popular West African drum known for its wide range of tones and rhythms.

Kora

A stringed instrument resembling a lute, originating from the Mandinka people.

Mbira

Also known as the thumb piano, this instrument is integral to Zimbabwean music.
